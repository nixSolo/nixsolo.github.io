There are no venture capitalists behind this app, and no corporate board. Just me. I am a solo developer, and I built The Siege entirely from scratch.

Rooted in behavioral psychology and science, this tool is designed to help you isolate a negative pattern and commit to breaking it. It isn't designed to make you feel bad about your habits, but it won't coddle you either. It requires honesty and presence.

This current version represents my end vision of how the foundation should work. From here, its evolution will be guided by you. I welcome your feedback, whether you have ideas for new mechanics, or simply want to suggest new themes and color combinations.

I sincerely hope this tool helps you reclaim your time and mind.

Privacy and Respect.

Your data is 100% local. What you write stays on your device. We do not collect your personal entries, and we do not send them to any servers. It is entirely private to you.

Unlike many apps, we refuse to shove ads in your face or lock the core experience behind a hard paywall. The primary functions of The Siege can be accessed without ever watching an ad or paying a cent. For free users, ad trackers are present, but rest assured: they only collect data related to delivering the ads, absolutely nothing regarding what you do inside the app.

You Are In Control

You decide when to interact with the ad system. Watching two short 30-second ads will unlock almost all premium features and completely hide the ad system for the next 24 hours. A seamless, uninterrupted experience on your terms.
