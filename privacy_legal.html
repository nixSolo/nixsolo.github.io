3. Data Privacy – Your Data Stays on Your Device

The Siege is built with a privacy-first philosophy.

For the current version (Version 1):

Local Storage Only

All journal entries, reflections, habit logs, streaks, and internal progress data are stored locally on your device using SQLite.

No Cloud Sync

The Siege does not upload, transmit, or store your journal entries or personal reflections on any external server.

No Account Required

The App does not require your name, email address, phone number, or personal identity information.

Anonymous Local Identifier

A random UUID is generated locally on your device to manage streaks and internal tracking. This identifier never leaves your device.

If you uninstall the App, your data is permanently deleted unless you manually export it beforehand.

The Siege itself does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal user data.

5. Data Deletion ("Kill Switch")

You maintain full control over your data.

At any time, you may permanently delete all local data by navigating to:

Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account

This action:

  • Deletes the local database
  • Removes all journal entries
  • Resets streaks
  • Cannot be undone

There is no server copy.
